Seeing the way that God has been faithful to work what appears to be a tragedy for our good and His glory has been the greatest source of joy and comfort. The gift of a son is pretty amazing as well.

When I wrote "Lead Me," I had no idea that God was preparing me to lead my family through a season of great adversity. It was only a month after Pieces of a Real Heart was released that my wife, Sarah, and I found out that our son had a severe, life-threatening heart defect.

I was writing songs because I needed them, songs about trusting God in difficult circumstances.

Serve your neighbors (like your next-door neighbors). It's a good way to get to know them better and show them what Jesus is about.
