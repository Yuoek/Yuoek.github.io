Hope is slowly extinguished and quickly revived.

Avarice is rarely the vice of youth.

Can mortal prayers ensure immortal happiness?

How we are born to invent our own miseries!

Benevolence, like religion, awes even those it cannot win.

Let us respect even error when it has its source in virtue.

How easy it is to be unknown!

Society, that first of blessings, brings with it evils death only can cure.
