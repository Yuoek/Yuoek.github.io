In a dead religion there are no more heresies.

Just as war is waged with the blood of others, fortunes are made with other people's money.

One is called a criminal for being different, and malicious for observing other people with too much clarity and penetration. But what if one began with oneself?

Heresy is the lifeblood of religions. It is faith that begets heresies. There are no heresies in a dead religion.
