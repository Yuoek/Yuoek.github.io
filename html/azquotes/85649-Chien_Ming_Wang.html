In Taiwan there's a saying: Raising a child is more important than giving birth. Raising a child is greater.

I was so happy when I found out I had been drafted by the Yankees. Growing up in Taiwan, I had heard so much about the Yankees but had never even seen them on TV.

Communicating is a bit of a problem. Playing in New York is great but there are times when I feel a long way from home.

The Yankees are one of the best teams. I want to stay with them forever.

I felt I had to work even harder in order to help two sets of parents. Most of my money I send home to let my parents manage. The rest I use for living expenses in America.
