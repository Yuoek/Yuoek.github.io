Mary Kay Ash Quotes

Never give up, because you never know if the next try is going to be the one that works.

Make every day count.
Make every hour count.
Make every minute count.
And don't stop until you have exercised your full potential, realizing your impossible dream and fulfilling your total destiny to become the person that you, and only you , are capable of being.

Every achievement, big or small, begins in your mind.

Don't limit yourself. Many people limit themselves to what they think they can do. You can go as far as your mind lets you. What you believe, remember, you can achieve.

There are three types of people in this world: those who make things happen, those who watch things happen and those who wonder what happened. We all have a choice. You can decide which type of person you want to be. I have always chosen to be in the first group.

Don't let the negatives of life control you. Rise above them. Use them as your stepping stones to go higher than you ever dreamed possible

Everyone wants to be appreciated, so if you appreciate someone, don't keep it a secret.

No matter how busy you are, you must take time to make the other person feel important.

Hope is wishing for something to come true. Faith is belief that it will come true. Believe that for every problem God gives you, He will also provide you with a solution.

Your attitude determines your altitude. It really is true that If you think you can, you can; and if you think you can’t – you’re right.

The definition of successful people is simply ordinary people with extraordinary determination. You cannot keep determined people from success. If you place stumbling blocks in their way, they will use them for stepping-stones and climb to new heights. People who succeed have a goal, a dream and make their plans and follow them.

For every failure, there's an alternative course of action. You just have to find it. When you come to a roadblock, take a detour.

God does not ask your ability Or your inability. He asks only your availability.

Whenever I meet someone, I try to imagine her wearing an invisible sign that says: MAKE ME FEEL IMPORTANT. I respond to this sign immediately, and it works wonders.

There is a way; Up, Around, Over or Through

Be a dreamer. Have a sense of greatness. It has been said that if you can dream it, you can do it. And I believe that. Before your dream can become a reality, you have to see it in your own mind; see its fulfillment, whatever it may be.

My definition of happiness is having something to do what you love to do, someone to love, and something to look forward to.

The only difference between successful people and unsuccessful people is extraordinary determination.

It is important for you to have a goal. You simply can't get there if you don't know where you are going. Begin to build in your mind a dream. The write it down and make your goal realistic. Aim high enough that you will have to stretch your ability and your potential to reach it.

The greatest pollution problem we face today is negativity. Eliminate the negative attitude and believe you can do anything. Replace 'if I can, I hope, maybe' with 'I can, I will, I must.

When you come to a roadblock, take a detour.

Do not desire to fit in. Desire to lead.

Dare to grow into your dreams and claim this as your motto: Let it be me.

What you believe, you can achieve.

Everyone has an invisible sign hanging from their neck saying, 'Make me feel important.' Never forget this message when working with people.
