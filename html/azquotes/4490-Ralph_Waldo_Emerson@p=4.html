Ralph Waldo Emerson Quotes - Page 4

The creation of a thousand forests is in one acorn.

Make the most of yourself, for that is all there is of you.

It is one of the blessings of old friends that you can afford to be stupid with them.

Every wall is a door.

Always do what you are afraid to do.

Freedom is not the right to live as we please, but the right to find how we ought to live in order to fulfill our potential.

Neither you nor the world knows what you can do until you have tried.

The civility of no race can be perfect whilst another race is degraded. It is a doctrine alike of the oldest and of the newest philosophy, that man is one, and that you cannot injure any member, without a sympathetic injury to all the members

Do not be too timid and squeamish about your actions. All life is an experiment. The more experiments you make the better. What if they are a little coarse and you may get your coat soiled or torn? What if you do fail, and get fairly rolled in the dirt once or twice? Up again, you shall never be so afraid of a tumble.

To believe your own thought, to believe that what is true for you in your private heart, is true for all men - that is genius... Whoso would be a man must be a nonconformist... What I must do, is all that concerns me; not what the people think... Nothing can bring you peace but yourself; nothing, but the triumph of principles.

It takes a good deal of character to judge a person by his future instead of his past

Live in the sunshine, swim the sea, drink the wild air.

We see the world piece by piece, as the sun, the moon, the animal, the tree; but the whole, of which these are shining parts, is the soul.

If you want to be great and successful, choose people who are great and successful and walk side by side with them.

Our strength grows out of our weakness.

Life is a perpetual instruction in cause and effect.

Each of us sees in others what we carry in our own hearts.

Little minds have little worries, big minds have no time for worries.

None of us will ever accomplish anything excellent or commanding except when he listens to this whisper which is heard by him alone.

The good news is that the moment you decide that what you know is more important than what you have been taught to believe, you will have shifted gears in your quest for abundance. Success comes from within, not from without.

The only way to have a friend is to be one.

The stupidity of men always invites the insolence of power.

What you do speaks so loud that I cannot hear what you say.

The days come and go but they say nothing, and if we do not use the gifts they bring, they carry them as silently away.

Enthusiasm is the engine of success.
