Rabindranath Tagore Quotes - Page 3

Love is an endless mystery, because there is no reasonable cause that could explain it.

The human soul is on its journey from the law to love, from discipline to liberation, from the moral plane to the spiritual.

The false can never grow into truth by growing in power.

Music is the purest form of art, and therefore the most direct expression of beauty, with a form and spirit which is one and simple, and least encumbered with anything extraneous. We seem to feel that the manifestation of the infinite in the finite forms of creation is music itself, silent and visible.

Trees are the earth's endless effort to speak to the listening heaven.

I seem to have loved you in numberless forms, numberless times,
in life after life, in age after age forever.
He who wants to do good, knocks at the gate;
He who loves, finds the door open.

Let not the hours pass by in the dark. Kindle the lamp of love with thy life.

Whatever we treasure for ourselves separates us from others; our possessions are our limitations.

Love gives beauty to everything it touches.

All that is not given is lost.

One who has never known the turbulence of life, in whom the petals of the mysterious flower within have never opened; such a one may seem happy, may seem a saint, his single track mind may impress the multitude with its power - but he is ill equipped for life's true adventure into the infinite.

The Taj Mahal rises above the banks of the river like a solitary tear suspended on the cheek of time.

Those who own much have much to fear.

Dark clouds become heaven's flowers when kissed by light.

I believe in a spiritual world - not as anything separate from this world - but as its innermost truth. With the breath we draw we must always feel this truth, that we are living in God.

Patriotism cannot be our final spiritual shelter; my refuge is humanity. I will not buy glass for the price of diamonds, and I will never allow patriotism to triumph over humanity as long as I live.

The water in a vessel is sparkling; the water in the sea is dark. The small truth has words which are clear; the great truth has great silence.

Only in silence I find myself. Life in the city is so hectic that you lose the right perspective. It's important to know that our biggest resources are in our heart.

The cure for all the illness of life is stored in the inner depth of life itself, the access to which becomes possible when we are alone. This solitude is a world in itself, full of wonders and resources unthought of. It is absurdly near; yet so unapproachably distant.

The real frienship is like fluorescence, it shines better when everything has darken.

The flower which is single need not envy the thorns that are numerous.

Truth looks tawdry when she is overdressed.

The truth comes as conqueror only because we have lost the art of receiving it as guest.

Your love keeps me afloat but will remain the anchor in my soul... I don't really have words to describe how romantic I find that. Your love is what keeps me going, but it's also the anchor that keeps me close to you. I love it!

He who is too busy doing good finds no time to be good.
