Andrea Bocelli Quotes

Destiny has a lot to do with it, but so do you. You have to persevere, you have to insist.

Like stars across the sky, we were born to shine.

Even in the most beautiful music, there are some silences, which are there so we can witness the importance of silence. Silence is more important than ever, as life today is full of noise. We speak a lot about environmental pollution but not enough about noise pollution.

A young pregnant wife has been hospitalized for a simple attack of appendicitis. The doctors had to apply ice to her stomach and when the treatments ended the doctors suggested that she abort the child, they told her it was the 'best solution' because the baby would be born with some disability but the young brave wife decided not to abort, and the child was born. That woman was my Mother and I was the child.

I was an agnostic until I realized that I had to choose between God and fate. The idea that humanity and nature are the result of fate was not convincing at all. I find the presence of God everywhere.

Music is able to make a person dream. When you dream, you dream of something good, something beautiful, and when you dream, you always dream of yourself better than you are.

Faith has a role to play in life. It is not related to success. It is the other way round. Success is related to faith. Faith comes first.

All that counts in life is intention.

I do not think anyone can ever do anything without the help and will of God.

The voice is something very mysterious. It's difficult to say what is inside a voice that moves people.

If you could have the arms of Hercules, legs as swift as the wind. If you could leap shoulder high above the rim, have the kick of a dolphin, the reflexes of a cat. If you could have all these, you would have the body, you would have the tools. But you would not have greatness until you understand that the strongest muscle is the heart. To me, that's the soul of the Olympic Games.

I want to have time for myself, my family and my friends. It's important because in order to sing well, you must have inspiration, and inspiration comes from life, from living.

Fatherhood is a very natural thing; it's not something that shakes up my life but rather it enriches it.

When I get on stage, my first goal is not to show my expertise, but on the contrary, to give a bit of happiness, of joy, of cheerfulness. I am firmly convinced that in order to sing well, you must love your neighbor and be passionate about life.

A career is like a house: it's made of many bricks, and each brick has the same value, because without any one of them, the house would collapse.

The nature of music is mysterious and so much so that it generates strong emotions within us. It moves along passages that reach the most intimate areas of our psyche without being tried by prejudices or influences of any kind.

Singing provides a true sense of lightheartedness. If I sing when I am alone, I feel wonderful. It's freedom.

Success is related to faith. Faith comes first.

When I was very young, I started trying to sing like the great tenor Mario Lanza; my family used to play his records. We all learn best by imitating others.

Living is dangerous. The important thing is to know the limits.

I live for my children, so my number one rule is I won't go away from home for more than two weeks.

In essence, I set myself the objective of doing what I feel is right without having any ambition.

Now I'm more sure and I feel myself more comfortable singing.

I have a European frame of mind and Europe is my home.

Because, in opera, I have to sing for people that are very far from me, instead of, when I sing a song, I try to imagine to sing like in an ear of a child.
