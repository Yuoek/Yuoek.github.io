I have always been interested in theatre and after reading the script for THE INFLICTION OF CRUELTY, I thought that being part of this show and the Fringe would be a great experience.

When I first moved to New York I had been trying to work on anything and everything I could. From extra work and commercials to short and student films so I could learn in front of a camera.

One day I received a call from my agent, he asked me to put myself on tape for a film that was being cast in LA. I was thrilled at the possibility alone.

Mainly, I feel very fortunate to be able to do what I love.

When I was in school I would try and take all my classes early in the morning or at night so that I would have most of the day to go out on auditions.

I decided to pursue an acting career after having had an incredible experience working on a play in high school.
