Andrea Bocelli Quotes - Page 4

An opera singer is like an athlete before a match. An athlete cannot overdo anything. In order to perform at the highest possible level, you need to refrain from activities so as to be able to express this power.

I was a daredevil before, and after I lost my sight I was the same. I loved riding bikes, scooters and horses. I even learned to box. Muhammad Ali is my hero.

I loved riding bikes and horses. I was eight when I started having lessons, and when my father bought me my own horse I couldn't wait to go off on my own.

When you sing and people want that you sing, then you can hope to be great.

I have always loved music and singing, and I am open to listen to any type of music. Regardless of my mood, my heart is always set racing when I listen to opera. When I decide which music I want to hear, my choice is almost invariably an opera recording.

I like to windsurf and ski, and most of all I love to ride horses. The wilder and faster the better! If I'm presented with a fast horse or a fast boat, I still get that shiver of excitement and I cannot resist. Luckily I never seem to have any accidents, and thank God for that.

I like reading... French, Russian classics - Gogol, Tolstoy, Dostoyevsky, Flaubert. I also like Hemingway, Virginia Woolf.

I don't like being called 'macho.' Macho basically means stupid and a real Italian man is not macho, he's smart. That's smart in both senses: elegant and clever.

I don't know if One Direction will stand the test of time. I have a niece who goes crazy for them. But the only way to judge art is to wait and see if it becomes evergreen. This takes a bit of time. Adele is a very good musician and I'd like to sing with her. But, again, time will tell if her music will become evergreen.

I started as a lyrical singer. But it was through the pop universe that I reached international fame.

It is not Christian to go against someone. I am in favour of life.

I change the language with which I use my voice. In opera, I know I have an orchestra behind me; I have to communicate to people very far from me.

I have to face life with a newly found passion. I must rediscover the irresistible will to learn, to live and to love.

The themes I sing about tend to be centered on love, but in the most total, general way 360 degrees of love.

I'm quite shy, so I hate seeing my private life splashed over the papers.

I loved playing football. In this particular match the ball happened to hit my right eye, the only one which I could see light and colour with.

I have trouble with modern art. But in general, all art forms fascinate me - art is the way human beings express what we can't say in words.

I know that my passion is for opera, but sometimes I like also to sing songs, because there are many beautiful melodies.

I don't like crying. I'm a country boy, and we're the product of our upbringing. As a boy, I was told that men don't cry.

I have big, big stage fright.

I was overcome with happiness, love, and tenderness ... Right after he was washed, I studied him with my hands: his downy little head, his little nose, everything on the tiny body.

Popularity gets up people's noses. But I understand the importance and the function of popular music. There is an artistic purpose. Popular music helps people to develop a curiosity and leads them towards classical music.

People are often quite surprised by the sport and leisure activities practised by the blind. For example, tandem cycling is very popular.

Some years ago I gave a concert in the mountains with snow all around, and that was much colder.

Losing my sight had nothing to do with my focus on music. My passion for music was already there, so it would be a mistake to give too much significance to my blindness.
