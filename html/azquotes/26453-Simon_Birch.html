Time is a monster that cannot be reasoned with. It responds like a snail to our impatience, then it races like a gazelle when you can't catch a breath.

What I want to do and what I do are two separate things. If we all went around doing what we wanted all the time, there'd be chaos.

When you lose someone, you don't lose them all at once. You lose them in pieces over time.

I don't need proof, I have faith.
