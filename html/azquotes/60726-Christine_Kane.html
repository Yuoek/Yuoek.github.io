When we say no, or when we follow our dreams or true callings - people might be disappointed in our choices. Allowing people their disappointments sets us free.

Feeling better is temporary. Transformation is forever.

No amount of trying to inhale one more shred of inspiration is going to make it any easier to put your heart and soul out there.

Your ego wants to move through life risk-free, foolish-free, discouragement-free, mistake-free, tired-free.

Living Empty means we release the things that are no longer in alignment with who we are becoming. The release itself tells us who that is. The emptiness shows us the way.
