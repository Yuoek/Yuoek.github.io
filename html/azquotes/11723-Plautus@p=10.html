Plautus Quotes - Page 10

A mouse relies not solely on one hole.

That's a miserable and cursed word, to say I had, when what I have is nothing.

He is happy in his wisdom who has learned at another's expense.

He is a friend indeed who proves himself a friend in need.

The Bell never rings of itself; unless some one handles or moves it it is dumb.
[Lat., Nunquam aedepol temere tinniit tintinnabulum;
Nisi quis illud tractat aut movet, mutum est, tacet.]

The mind is hopeful; success is in God's hands.
[Lat., Sperat quidem animus: quo eveniat, diis in manu est.]

What is yours is mine, and all mine is yours.

He whom the Gods love dies young.

This is the great evil in wine, it first seizes the feet; it is a cunning wrestler.
[Lat., Magnum hoc vitium vino est,
Pedes captat primum; luctator dolosu est.]

We only appreciate the comforts of life in their loss.

He who tries to protect himself from deception is often cheated, even when most on his guard.

In wondrous ways do the gods make sport with men.
[Lat., Miris modis Di ludos faciunt hominibus.]

What is thine is mine, and all mine is thine.
[Lat., Quod tuum'st meum'st; omne meum est autem tuum.]

Fortune moulds and circumscribes human affairs as she pleases.
[Lat., Fortuna humana fingit artatque ut lubet.]

To blow and to swallow at the same time is not easy; I cannot at the same time be here and also there.
[Lat., Simul flare sorbereque haud facile
Est: ego hic esse et illic simul, haud potui.]

Modesty becomes a young man.
[Lat., Adolescentem verecundum esse decet.]

He who would eat the kernel, must crack the shell.
[Lat., Qui e nuce nucleum esse vult, frangat nucem.]

Courage in danger is half the battle.
[Lat., Bonus animus in mala re, dimidium est mali.]

He is hailed a conqueror of conquerors.
[Lat., Victor victorum cluet.]

If you spend a thing you can not have it.
[Lat., Non tibi illud apparere si sumas potest.]

I have lost my oil and my labor. (Labored in vain.)
[Lat., Oleum et operam perdidi.]

It is our human lot, it is heaven's will, that sorrow follow joy.

It is difficult to whistle and drink at the same time.

Flame is very near to smoke.

Spice a dish with love and it pleases every palate.
