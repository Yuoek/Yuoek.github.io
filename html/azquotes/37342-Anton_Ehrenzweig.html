Abstract art has helped us to experience the emotional power inherent in pure form.

Every student deserves to be treated as a potential genius.

Superficially insignificant or accidental looking detail [in art] may well carry the most important unconscious symbolism.

The work of art

The immortality of great art seems bound up with the inevitable loss of its original surface meaning and its rebirth in the spirit of every new age.
