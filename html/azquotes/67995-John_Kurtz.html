Artists paint not for their own enjoyment but to share their vision with others.

Artists never seem to get bored with life.

The love of art in its many forms lives on and enables us to enjoy the past, the present, and look with confidence to the future.

How can you measure the contribution that artists make to society?

Artists are always searching for beauty - and when they find it and fashion it in their own unique way - it does not seem too important to analyze how it came about.
