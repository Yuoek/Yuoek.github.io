God loves us the way we are, but too much to leave us that way.

There is no detour to holiness, Jesus came to the resurrection through the cross, not around it.

True leadership means to receive power from God and use it under God's rule to serve people in God's way.

Often in the morning I will sit in a favorite chair in my study with a cup of coffee, with classical music playing, not trying to form a prayer with words but waiting, listening, until perhaps I sense the Spirit bringing to the surface a word from God. Then I offer just a simple 'Thank you.'

Evangelism is a cross in the heart of God.
