There is never a lack of subject matter; just absence of creativity.

To live an art-filled life, one must be willing to try new things & accept that things change.

A true artist is driven by the never-ending desire to create.

The more you put yourself out there, the more there is for people to take exception with.

Fear of failure and criticism can be crippling. Don't let others' negativity take away your joy.

If it was easy, everyone would be doing it. That's why they call it artWORK!

It's possible to have an awesome life, but it's up to ourselves to do it.
