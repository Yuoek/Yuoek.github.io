Avoid the crowd. Do your own thinking independently. Be the chess player, not the chess piece.

It is through cooperation, rather than conflict, that your greatest successes will be derived.

The inner speech, your thoughts, can cause you to be rich or poor, loved or unloved, happy or unhappy, attractive or unattractive, powerful or weak.

Nobody succeeds beyond his or her wildest expectations unless he or she begins with some wild expectations.

Do more than you have to do, more than your share, and do it as well as you can.
