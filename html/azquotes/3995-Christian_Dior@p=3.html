Christian Dior Quotes - Page 3

Without foundation, there can be no fashion.

I had the luck at 18 to become assistant to Christian Dior, and to succeed him at 21 and to meet with success from my first collection in 1958. That will be 44 years in a few days. Above all it was Christian Dior who was my master and who was the first to reveal the secrets and mysteries of haute couture.

The only queen I ever dressed was Eva Perón.

Of the women in my childhood, I retain above all the memory of their perfumes, perfumes that lingered - filling the lift with fragrance long after they had gone.

My dream is to save them from nature.

In a machine age, dressmaking is one of the last refuges of the human, the personal, the inimitable.
