I think it's always important to put your own spin and bring your essence to whatever character you play.

I think I am most comfortable when I am on stage. I feel free, like I can be or do anything and it feels like home.

It's always fun to see where my journey takes me and how sometimes what's not on the list is so much better than what you could have imagined.

I love telling good stories and I love playing powerful, strong women.

I love hanging out with friends and family, going to the beach or just being a couch potato and binge watching TV shows or watching a good movie.

With performing live, you obviously don't get the luxury of another take, but for me, it is all the same.
