Other people write about the bling and the booty. I write about the pus and the gnats. To me, that's beautiful.

A lot of my songs are very personal, always, but this one felt like a memoir. I almost called it Hallucinated Memoir. "Granny" is a hallucinated memoir. It's straight-up symbolism for my life, in many ways.

My songs are very much a kind of psychoanalysis. I am very introspective in my songs, and I am working through, always.
