I'm secretly a clown, or maybe it's not much of a secret! I'm a little putzo, as the Italians say - a little bit loopy.

I am a total geek. I'm not even a closet comic book geek. I am the comic book geek.

Since I've been in an actor, I've lived in Italy, in London, in Stockholm - I had the fortune of working in different locations. If you live someplace long enough, you acquire slightly different systems of thought, and it influences your outlook on life. I just slowly adapted the way I speak.

I went to theater school but never really got the chance to do theater, and it's always been a dream of mine.
