Love is our highest word and the synonym of God.

For everything you have missed, you have gained something else, and for everything you gain, you lose something else.

In spite of warnings, change rarely occurs until the status quo becomes more painful than change. People seem not to see that their opinion of the world is also a confession of their character.

We acquire the strength we have overcome.

If a man's eye is on the Eternal, his intellect will grow.

If we encounter a man of rare intellect, we should ask him what books he reads.

Continual failure is a road to success - if you have the strength to go on.

The meaning of good and bad, of better and worse, is simply helping or hurting.

We live in succession, in division, in parts, in particles. Meantime within man is the soul of the whole; the wise silence; the universal beauty, to which every part and particle is equally related; the eternal ONE.

The next best thing to saying a good thing yourself is to quote one.

Nothing is more simple than greatness; indeed, to be simple is to be great.

A man's library is a sort of harem.

You cannot see the mountain near.

Though we travel the world over to find the beautiful, we must carry it with us or we find it not.

A child reminds us that playtime is an essential part of our daily routine.

Thoughts rule the world.

There is a time in every man's education when he arrives at the conviction that envy is ignorance; that imitation is suicide; that he must take himself for better, for worse, as his portion; that though the wide universe is full of good, no kernel of nourishing corn can come to him but through his toil bestowed on that plot of ground which is given to him to till. The power which resides in him is new in nature, and none but he knows what that is which he can do, nor does he know until he has tried.

What your heart thinks is great, is great. The soul's emphasis is always right.

To the poet, to the philosopher, to the saint, all things are friendly and sacred, all events profitable, all days holy, all men divine.

What we have learned from other becomes our own reflection.

Sitting back in the evening, stargazing and stroking your dog, is an infallible remedy.

All life is an experiment.

An ounce of action is worth a ton of theory.

Desire is possibility seeking expression.

That which dominates our imagination and our thoughts will determine our life and character.
