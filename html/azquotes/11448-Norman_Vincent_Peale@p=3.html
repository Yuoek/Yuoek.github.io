Action is a great restorer and builder of confidence. Inaction is not only the result, but the cause, of fear. Perhaps the action you take will be successful; perhaps different action or adjustments will have to follow. But any action is better than no action at all.

Positive Thinkers get positive results because they appreciate the inestimable value of a day, this day, not the next day, but this day, and every day. Today offers at least sixteen waking hours that may be crammed FULL of opportunity, joy, excitement, and achievement.

Throw back the shoulders, let the heart sing, let the eyes flash, let the mind be lifted up, look upward and say to yourself ... nothing is impossible!

Trouble is here. It is for a purpose. Use it for the purpose for which it was intended - to help you grow. Thank God for your troubles.

Nothing is more confusing than people who give good advice but set a bad example.

Every problem has in it the seeds of its own solution. If you don't have any problems, you don't get any seeds.

When you expect the best you release a magnetic force in your mind which by a law of attraction tends to bring the best to you.

The person who sends out positive thoughts activates the world around him positively and draws back to himself positive results.

First thing every morning before you arise say out loud, I believe.

Happiness will never come if it's a goal in itself; happiness is a by-product of a commitment of worthy causes.

Live your life and forget your age.

If you want a quality, act as if you already have it. If you want to be courageous, act as if you were - and as you act and persevere in acting, so you tend to become.

We are all capable of
greater things than we realize.

Become a possibilitarian. No matter how dark things seem to be or actually are, raise your sights and see possibilities -- always see them, for they're always there.

How you think about a problem is more important than the problem itself. So always think positively.

To be successful is to be helpful, caring and constructive, to make everything and everyone you touch a little bit better.

Learn to relax. Your body is precious, as it houses your mind and spirit. Inner peace begins with a relaxed body.

People who have the courage to be individuals can usually think things through on their own and make sound decisions. They don't say, "What will people think?" They say, "What's the best way to handle this?" The amazing fact is that God created each one of us as a separate, unique person amid billions of other separate, unique individuals. So the best way to achieve real fulfillment is to be yourself.

There are two primary forces in this world, fear and faith. Fear can move you to destructiveness or sickness or failure. Only in rare instances will it motivate you to accomplishment. But faith is a greater force. Faith can drive itself into your consciousness and set you free from fear forever.

If you want to get somewhere you have to know where you want to go and how to get there. Then never, never, never give up.

A basic law: the more you practice the art of thankfulness, the more you have to be thankful for.

Know that you yourself are a miracle.

Put your trust in God and just go calmly on your way.

Never react emotionally to criticism. Analyze yourself to determine whether it is justified. If it is, correct yourself. Otherwise, go on about your business.
