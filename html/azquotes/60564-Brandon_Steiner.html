Occasionally when you’re in that zone, striving to operate at your highest levels, you get consumed. You forget to eat and shower. But that can be a good thing. No one makes money eating or showering.

If you want more money, don't pay attention to the money. Pay attention to the thing that makes the money.

Being generous without keeping score strengthens your spirit, keeps you focused on the people who make your business what it is, and helps breed success.
