It's not harder to hear the inner voice when you're in a relationship, but it's more of a challenge to follow it.

I think of the inner voice as a compass or as a wayshower to guide our path.

Using your intuition is such a wonderful way to live. It's very exciting.

We have a lot of controlling people who are trying to tell us how to live our lives, and a lot of co-dependent people around us. We bump up against these people, too, when we're trying to get on the spiritual path.

I've come to believe, on the journey of mine, that we have a still, small voice - and that voice does come from God. It's there to bring us comfort and to bring us guidance with everything.

Intuition is something all of us have been given.
