He who opens a school door, closes a prison.

They say love is blindness of heart; I say not to love is blindness.

You preserve your shame but you kill your glory.

It is by suffering that human beings become angels.

How did it happen that their lips came together? How does it happen that birds sing, that snow melts, that the rose unfolds, that the dawn whitens behind the stark shapes of trees on the quivering summit of the hill? A kiss, and all was said.

Each of our passions, even love, has a stomach that must not be overloaded. We must in everything write the word 'finis' in time; we must restrain ourselves, when it becomes urgent; we must draw the bolt on the appetite, play a fantasia on the violin, then break the strings with our own hand. The Wise man is he who knows when and how to stop.

Curiosity is one of the forms of feminine bravery.

Conscience is God present in man.

To sum up all, let it be known that science and religion are two identical words. The learned do not suspect this, no more do the religious. These two words express the two sides of the same fact, which is the infinite. Religion-Science, this is the future of the human mind.

Life is the flower for which love is the honey.

Youth is the future smiling at a stranger, which is itself.

Love is reducing the universe to one being.

What makes night within us may leave stars.

God knows better than we do what we need.

The sewer is the conscience of the city.

Emotion is always new.

Doing nothing is happiness for children and misery for old men.

England has two books, the Bible and Shakespeare. England made Shakespeare,but the Bible made England.

The three great problems of this century; the degradation of man in the proletariat, the subjection of women through hunger, the atrophy of the child by darkness.

When a man understands the art of seeing, he can trace the spirit of an age and the features of a king even in the knocker on a door.

Love each other dearly always. There is scarcely anything else in the world but that: to love one another.

Man's greatest actions are performed in minor struggles. Life, misfortune, isolation, abandonment and poverty are battlefields which have their heroes - obscure heroes who are at times greater than illustrious heroes.

Youth, even in its sorrows, always has a brilliancy of its own.
