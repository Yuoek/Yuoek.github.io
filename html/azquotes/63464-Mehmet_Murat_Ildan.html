Mehmet Murat Ildan Quotes

A wise quote can only change a wise man! Therefore, wise sayings are for the wise men, not for the fools! The sunflowers turn their face toward the Sun, the fools, toward the darkness!

Every man is a door; when the door is closed, just search for the key gently! Remember that every door has a key!

Life is a miracle; walking is a miracle; watching the sunset is a miracle; everything is a miracle, because existence is a miracle!

Visit the graveyards sometimes and read the headstone epitaphs! There is much to learn from the dark face of the life!

In matters of goodness, never expect any compliment or appreciation; just do your goodness and disappear!

Don't look for an unshakable rock to stand on; there ain't any! Rather, learn how to stand still on a shaking rock!

The system wants you to be either a bow or an arrow; refuse both, because there is a third choice: To be an archer!

Do not underrate others, do not underrate yourself, and do not underrate anything! Do not overrate others, do not overrate yourself, and do not overrate anything!

In deep waters, you encounter only the wise and the brave; in shallow waters, the ignorant and the coward!

In hard times, gold is a good friend and good friend is gold!

The power of every dictator has two main sources: His psychopathic mind and the support of the very easily deceivable ignorant masses!

Do not hold an idea tightly! There is always a possibility that what you believe might be wrong! Hold your ideas loosely so that you can easily get rid of them once you have realised that they are wrong!

Behind every success, there lies a magical word: Dare!

If your friend goes to a far place, that far place comes close to you!

Everyday, spend some time in solitude to repair the damages done by the crowds!

Help is not in the sky, help is within you, in your mind; help is your reason! Turn your face to your reason not to the sky!

If you want to appreciate the Sun, be a miner; to value the land, be a sailor and to long for freedom, get married...

In a democracy, people always vote for their alike! Pig for the pig, raven for the raven! Dull for the dull, wise for the wise!

If you have discovered a truth, tell it first to a parrot! Every new truth needs an insistent repetition!

If heaven is within you, everywhere you go you will find heaven!

I have not seen any Christian sparrow or Muslim lion or Jewish elephant! Animals are lucky as they have not yet invented the religion!

When you come across with a problem in your life, do not always try to solve it; make a long jump like a kangaroo and continue your way! Sometimes problems must be leaped over without touching them!

Ignorant voters of the democracies are always a great threat to the progression of humanity, simply because they give their votes to the people who look like themselves!

A little happy house is the strongest castle in this whole universe!

If you have good friends, don't look for treasure because you are already rich; if you have just treasure, look for good friends, because you are still poor!
