We are living in a cultural dark age of musical pollution. You put the radio on, and five minutes later you need an aspirin.

I function as a channel from which music emerges from the chaos of noise.

Mythology, science and space exploration are subjects that have fascinated me since my early childhood. And they were always connected somehow with the music I write.

I am merely a conduit, a kind of big hairy tool. I am just a plastic funnel connected to a Moog.
