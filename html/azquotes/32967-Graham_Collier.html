The influence of a beautiful, helpful character is contagious, and may revolutionize a whole town.

Skill is less important than awareness.

The artist is a man who finds that the form or shape of things externally corresponds, in some strange way, to the movements of his mental and emotional life.

Effective as perspective is... it becomes a deadening influence on an artist's natural way of seeing things once it is accepted as a system - as a mechanical formula.
