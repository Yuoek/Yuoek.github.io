Mooji Quotes

Don't remind the world that it is sick and troubled. Remind it that it is beautiful and free.

Your heart is the light of this world. Don't cover it with your mind.

Don't be too quick to interpret the moment. Just keep quiet. My encouragement would always be: never think anything is against you, everything is blessing. Why should it be different? Just be quiet. Let it all work itself out.

No thought has any power. You have power. And when you identify and believe in the thought you give power to the thought.

All the ups and downs are grace in different wrappings, sent to refine consciousness. Say thanks to them all.

When you can bear your own silence, you are free.

The greatest healing would be to wake up from what we are not.

At the end of the day, you can have something in your head, something in your hand, or something in your heart. What are you choosing?

Some beings will walk with you for the duration of this bodily existence, up to the very end. Some will come with bright promises, bright lights, but they fade quickly. Others come, they don’t look like they will go very far, but they are marathon runners; they’re there with you all the time. You cannot determine this... Somehow in the flow of your own unique river, you will see that everything is as it should be.

Who reminds you to breathe and to beat your heart? Something is there, taking care.

You need nothing to be happy - you need something to be sad.

In the land of "I know," there is always competitiveness, jealousy, pretence, pride and arrogance. It is an aggressive realm - the realm of the ego. I say refuse citizenship. In the land of "I dont know," the inhabitants move without conflict and are naturally quiet, happy and peaceful. The wise stay here.

You already are, the peace you are looking for. Be still and know that

Your mantra is thank you. Just keep saying thank you. Don't explain. Don't complain. Just say thank you. Say thank you to existence.

Life is so much wiser and kinder than your mind imagines. Trust & Be Still.

When you don't want to be interesting, you are free.

If you seek approval from others in this world, you will not know happiness.

Let the world call you lazy for not running about like a frightened ghost. Just be quiet inside yourself. Don't bother about knowing how things should be and simply begin observing without prejudice, projections or desires. Notice how life flows of its own accord. Nothing here is a chaos, but a harmony. You are already inside this flow.

There is a presence, a silence, a stillness which is here by itself. There is no doer of it, no creator of this stillness. It is simply here in you, with you. It is the fragrance of your own self. There is nothing to do about this, it is naturally present. This fragrance of peace, this spaciousness, it is the fragrance of your own being.

It's not what the mind says.
It's how seriously it is taken.

Life cannot be against you, for you are Life itself. Life can only seem to go against the ego's projections, which are rarely the truth.

If you do not trust life to unfold, the mind takes over and it becomes a game of strategy, motivated by anxiety. This mistrust is unfair. Life has given us so much, and yet we do not trust it.

If you could look inside the Heart of any and every single human being, you would fall in love with them completely. If you see the inside as it really Is and not as your mind projects it to be, you would be so purely in love with the whole thing.

Go beyond everything. Don't collect anything.
A king does not need to go shopping in his own
kingdom. Nor does he beg.
Remember, you are the inner reality-pure awareness only.
All that arises are appearances in consciousness.
Don't bother with all that. Rest only as the awareness.
This is the secret.
