Life is not a journey you want to make on autopilot.

Life, even in the hardest times, is full of moments to savor. They will not come this way again, not in this way.

In this place of gracious uncertainty, we wait. For the broken places to be brought back together. For the meaning of our suffering to be revealed in his. For the righteous reign of a mighty God, whose goodness we will spend all eternity celebrating. We wait - with open, expectant hearts.

Letting ourselves be loved by God is the place where we stumble upon trust.

Have you thought that your willingness to forgive is really your affirmation of the power of God to do you good?
