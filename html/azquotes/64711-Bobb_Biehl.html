Without focusing and getting to clarity you cannot lead. You cannot motivate. You cannot plan. You cannot communicate.

Leadership is knowing what to do next, knowing why that's important, and knowing how to bring the appropriate resources to bear on the need at hand.

Without an adequate answer to the question, "Why?" the price is always to high.

You are capable of more than you realize. You are far more capable than you were even 12 months ago. Next year you will be able to do things you can't imagine doing today.

One of the very best ways to keep in touch with your own ups and downs is to keep track of what activities energize you and which drain you - on a regular basis.
