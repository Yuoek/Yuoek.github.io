Oscar Wilde Quotes - Page 3

Most people are other people. Their thoughts are someone else's opinions, their lives a mimicry, their passions a quotation.

A good friend will always stab you in the front.

People may fail many times, but they become failures only when they begin to blame someone else. Always forgive your enemies; nothing annoys them so much.

If you cannot prove a man wrong, don't panic. You can always call him names.

True contentment is not having everything, but in being satisfied with everything you have.

I love to talk about nothing. It's the only thing I know anything about.

Live! Live the wonderful life that is in you! Let nothing be lost upon you. Be always searching for new sensations. Be afraid of nothing.

To give and not expect return, that is what lies at the heart of love.

Hear no evil, speak no evil, and you won't be invited to cocktail parties.

A passion for pleasure is the secret of remaining young.

I don't want to go to heaven. None of my friends are there.

Two men look out a window. One sees mud, the other sees the stars.

I am so clever that sometimes I don't understand a single word of what I am saying.

Learn to differentiate between ignorance and stupidity.

Don't be discouraged if your children reject your advice. Years later they will offer it to their own offspring.

The old believe everything, the middle-aged suspect everything, the young know everything.

Ordinary riches can be stolen; real riches cannot. In your soul are infinitely precious things that cannot be taken from you.

In war, the strong make slaves of the weak, and in peace the rich makes slaves of the poor.

To define is to limit.

It is perfectly possible to get what you think you want and be miserable. It's possible too, to never get it but deeply enjoy the process of trying. In this world, there are only two tragedies. One is not getting what one wants, and the other is getting it.

Bigamy is having one wife too many. Monogamy is the same.

Ultimately the bond of all companionship, whether in marriage or in friendship, is conversation.

Death must be so beautiful. To lie in the soft brown earth, with the grasses waving above one's head, and listen to silence. To have no yesterday, and no tomorrow. To forget time, to forgive life, to be at peace.

Just be your self. Everybody else is already taken.

Disobedience, in the eyes of any one who has read history, is man's original virtue. It is through disobedience that progress has been made, through disobedience and through rebellion.
