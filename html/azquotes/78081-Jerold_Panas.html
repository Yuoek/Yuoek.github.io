What you do for yourself is fleeting and dies with you. What you do for others has unending benefit and is eternal.

Life is a wheel. The more you give, the more you get back.

Philanthropy is the rent we pay for the joy and privilege we have for our space on this earth.

Few actions of consequence in the world have been accomplished without passion.

When we come into this life, we don't really own anything. And we own nothing when we leave. It is only a lease we have during our lifetime, and it is up to us to make the most of it.

It's hard to outdo the Lord in generosity.

Excellence is never an accident.
