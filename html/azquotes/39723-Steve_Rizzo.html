Don't wait for the storms of your life to pass. Learn to dance in the rain.

We have created a mindset in our society where everyone wants what they want when they want it. And if we don't get what we want when we want it, we feel ripped off. To make matters worse, we intensify our problems by continuously rehashing our woe-is-me story to the entire world. Whatever it is that has the potential to keep you from enjoying the day, understand that it's not the situation itself that is causing you to be unhappy. It's your thoughts and how you allow them to control you. It's what you choose to focus on that fuels your emotions and defines your reality.

Life is about growing, learning, and becoming. You can not grow, learn, or become if you can not embrace the changes in your life.

What you think is what you get.

If you let fear of the unknown stop you from taking chances, you will stifle your true potential.

When you shift your focus, you change your life.

Everyone has an inner hero that they have glimpsed from time to time, just waiting to break free Now is the time to tap into your true hero potential.

It was always the power of love that pulled us through, and it was the power of laughter that kept us from falling apart.
