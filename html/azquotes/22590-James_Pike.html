If a person lacks self- acceptance, he can't live with himself; if he lacks self-criticism, others can't live with him.

The eleven o'clock hour on Sunday is the most segregated hour in American life.

We put ourselves there. The door to hell is locked from the inside.

It is disgraceful that people are being barred from neighborhoods and clubs on a basis that would have barred Jesus Himself.

Remember that life is not measured in hours but in accomplishments.
