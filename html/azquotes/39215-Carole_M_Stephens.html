We each belong to and are needed in the family of God.

It isn't enough to just be on the journey; we must be awake to our duty and continue with faith as we draw upon the comforting, strengthening, enabling, and healing power of the Atonement.

We're all at different places on the path. But we can work together to help each other 'press forward with a steadfastness in Christ, having a perfect brightness of hope, and a love of God and of all men.'

Earthly families all look different. And while we do the best we can to create strong traditional families, membership in the family of God is not contingent upon any kind of status - marital status, parental status, financial status, social status, or even the kind of status we post on social media.

Whenever a woman strengthens the faith of a child, she contributes to the strength of a family - now and in the future.

God will keep His promises to you as you honor your covenants with Him.

The Savior knows the difficulties of the way and can guide us through whatever sorrows and disappointments may come.

We are beloved spirit sons and daughters of heavenly parents, with a divine nature and destiny. Our Savior, Jesus Christ, loved us enough to give His life for us. His Atonement provides the way for us to progress on the path to our heavenly home, through sacred priesthood ordinances and covenants.

We all need each other. Sons of god need daughters of god, daughters of god need sons of god.
