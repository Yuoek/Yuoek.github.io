The mind, once stretched by a new idea, never returns to its original dimensions.

To laugh often and love much; to win the respect of intelligent persons and the affection of children; to earn the approbation of honest critics and endure the betrayal of false friends; to appreciate beauty.

Guard well your spare moments. They are like uncut diamonds. Discard them and their value will never be known. Improve them and they will become the brightest gems in a useful life.

Win as if you were used to it, lose as if you enjoyed it for a change.

Don't choose the better person, choose the person who makes a better you.

Character is higher than intellect... A great soul will be strong to live, as well as strong to think.

For every minute you remain angry, you give up sixty seconds of peace of mind.

The lover of nature is he whose inward and outward senses are still truly adjusted to each other; who has retained the spirit of infancy even into the era of manhood. His intercourse with heaven and earth, becomes part of his daily food. In the presence of nature, a wild delight runs through the man, in spite of real sorrows.

Happiness is a perfume you cannot pour on others without getting some on yourself.

You cannot do a kindness too soon, for you never know how soon it will be too late.

When a happy person comes into the room, it is as if another candle has been lit.

Before we acquire great power we must acquire wisdom to use it well.

I suffer whenever I see that common sight of a parent or senior imposing his opinion and way of thinking and being on a young soul to which they are totally unfit. Cannot we let people be themselves, and enjoy life in their own way? You are trying to make that man another you. One's enough.

Write it on your heart that every day is the best day in the year.

Nobody can bring you peace but yourself.

If I have lost confidence in myself, I have the universe against me.

Allow yourself to trust joy and embrace it. You will find you dance with everything.

Nothing external to you has any power over you.

The highest revelation is that God is in every man.

The most dangerous thing is illusion.

Insist upon yourself. Be original.

Nothing great was ever achieved without enthusiasm.

Successful people live well, laugh often, and love much. They've filled a niche and accomplished tasks so as to leave the world better than they found it, while looking for the best in others, and giving the best they have.
