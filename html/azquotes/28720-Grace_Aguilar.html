Truth is the vital breath of Beauty; Beauty the outward form of Truth.

For prayer is the language of the heart.

Every Hebrew should look upon his Faith as a temple extending over every land to prove the immutability of God and the unity of His purposes.

The Real [is] the sole foundation of the Ideal.

Sympathy is the charm of human life.

Poetry is a dangerous gift.

Oh, that in religion, as in everything else, man would judge his brother man by his own heart; and as dear, as precious as his peculiar creed may be to him, believe so it is with the faith of his brother!
