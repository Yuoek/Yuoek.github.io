There are many animation films out there for teens, tweens and family but there are not that many real life stories.

I never read a book if I'm auditioning for a film based on it. I like to stick to the script only because that's exactly what I have to play.

I love kidding around and having a good time.

I hadn't thought about the fact that I was a foster child but, did notice that (in films) I'm often without a mother or father or both.

I'm not one who likes rehearsing too much.

I love acting and I hope I'm fortunate enough to get good, interesting roles and I hope directors and producers will keep giving me the opportunity to act as I really love doing this.

Since I'm not a method actor, I don't actually feel angry or feel that I'm being mean, I just do it because I'm acting the part, doing my job and playing the role.

I will always love gymnastics.
