Mother Teresa Quotes - Page 7

It is a kingly act to assist the fallen.

Loneliness is the leprosy of the modern world.

Let us not use bombs and guns to overcome the world. Let us use love and compassion. Peace begins with a smile.

Do not wait for leaders; do it alone, person to person.

The openness of our hearts and minds can be measured by how wide we draw the circle of what we call family.

It is very important that children learn from their fathers and mothers how to love one another- not in the school, not from the teacher, but from you. It is very important that you share with your children the joy of that smile. There will be misunderstandings; every family has its cross, its suffering. Always be the first to forgive with a smile. Be cheerful, be happy.

We have very little, so we have nothing to be preoccupied with. The more you have, the more you are occupied, the less you give. But the less you have, the more free you are.

Love is proved by deeds; the more they cost us, the greater the proof of our love.

If you are kind, people may accuse you of ulterior motives. Be kind anyway.

Unless a life is lived for others, it is not worthwhile.

We have not come into the world to be numbered; we have been created for a purpose; for great things: to love and be loved.

We are not here to be successful, we are here to be FAITHFUL

I will never tire of repeating this: what the poor need the most is not pity but love. They need to feel respect for their human dignity, which is neither less nor different from the dignity of any other human being.

There are some people who, in order not to pray use as an excuse the fact that life is so hectic that it prevents us from praying. This cannot be. Prayer does not demand that we interrupt our work, but that we continue working as if it were a prayer. It is not necessary to always be in meditation, nor to consciously experience the sensation that we are talking to God, no matter how nice that would be. What matters is being with Him, living in Him, in His will. To love with a pure heart, to love everybody, especially to live the poor, is a twenty-four hour prayer.

Spread love everywhere you go; first of all in your house. Give love to your children, to your wife or husband, to a next door neighbor. Let no one ever come to you without leaving better and happier.

We must make our homes centers of compassion and forgive endlessly.

It is a poverty to decide that a child must die so that you may live as you wish.

It's not about how much you do, but how much love you put into what you do that counts. Life isn't worth living, unless lived for other people.

Be kind to each other. It is better to commit faults with gentleness than to work miracles with unkindness.

If you are successful, you will win some false friends & some true enemies: Succeed anyway

We shall never know all the good that a simple smile can do.

God loves me. I'm not here just to fill a place, just to be a number. He has chosen me for a purpose. I know it.

God has created us to love and to be loved, and this is the beginning of prayer-to know that He loves me, that I have been created for greater things.

I once picked up a woman from a garbage dump and she was burning with fever; she was in her last days and her only lament was: My son did this to me. I begged her: You must forgive your son. In a moment of madness, when he was not himself, he did a thing he regrets. Be a mother to him, forgive him. It took me a long time to make her say: I forgive my son. Just before she died in my arms, she was able to say that with a real forgiveness. She was not concerned that she was dying. The breaking of the heart was that her son did not want her. This is something you and I can understand.

Words which do not give the light of Christ increase the darkness.
