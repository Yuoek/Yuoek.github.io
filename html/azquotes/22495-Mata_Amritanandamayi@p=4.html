Mata Amritanandamayi Quotes - Page 4

Surrender and living in the present are one and the same.

If you see deficiencies in others, your duty is to forgive instead of pointing at it.

Real love is the complete absence of any negative feelings towards anyone.

Lessen the quantity of unnecessary thoughts and allow more space for the energy of love to flow within you.

If we keep holding on to God, there is nothing to fear.

Be content and happy with whatever comes to you.

When love and compassion fills your heart, you find newness in every moment, and life becomes a celebration.

Do no talk while eating.

Look upon others as being part of yourself.

No one becomes pure and selfless overnight; it involves time and concentrated effort, coupled with tremendous patience and love.

The guru is not someone who is confined to the body. When there is selfless love for the guru, we will be able to see him not only in his body but in every living and nonliving thing in this world. Learn to see everything as the guru's body and to serve them accordingly.

Many people meditate in order that a third eye may open. For that they feel they should close their two physical eyes. They thereby become blind to the world. But the fact is that the third eye will never open. We can never close our eyes to the world in the name of spirituality. Self-realization is the ability to see ourselves in all beings. This is the third eye through which you see, even while your two eyes are open. We should be able to love and serve others, seeing ourselves in them. This is the fulfillment of spiritual practice.

Thankfulness is a humble, open and prayerful attitude that helps you receive more of God's grace.

True happiness is when the love that is within us finds expression in external activities.

Pure love is the best medicine for the modern world. This is what is lacking in all societies. The root cause of all problems, from personal problems to global problems is the absence of love. Love is the binding factor, the unifying factor. Love creates the feeling of oneness among people. It unifies a nation and its people. Love creates a sense of unity while hatred causes division. Egotism and hatred cuts people's minds into pieces. Love should rule. There is no problem which love cannot solve.

Married life, if lived with the proper love and understanding, helps awaken the feminine within a man, and the masculine within a woman.

Transcending the cycle of death and rebirth is the real purpose of this life in human form.

By living in harmony with Nature one gains a healthy mind and body.

Keep a constant awareness and a conscious effort to say good words, perform good actions, and to practice patience and compassion.

It should be for satsang that we go to spiritual centers. By going there, people who are involved in the world can attain peace and concentration. The concentration gained when one goes there cannot be achieved if one sits at home. Even though the breeze blows everywhere, coolness will be felt more if we sit in the shade of a tree. In the same way, although God is all-pervading, this presence will clearly shine in certain places more than others. That is the greatness of satsang. Satsang is the best thing for spiritual advancement.

Look carefully at what is of value in others and respect that.

To awaken spiritual unity, and to spread to others the love that is our inherent nature, is the true goal of human life.

Just as food and sleep are necessary for the body, spiritual understanding is needed for a healthy mind.

Society is made up of individuals. The thoughts and actions of each individual influence the culture of that society. Instead of waiting for others to improve, we should try to improve ourselves. Once our attitude has changed, we will be able to perceive goodness throughout world. If there is a positive change in us, it will also be reflected in others. It is only what we give that we can hope to get back.

What we need is peace of mind. We can gain that only through the control of our mind.
