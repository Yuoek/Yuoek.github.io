The little space within the heart is as great as the vast universe.
The heavens and the earth are there, and the sun and the moon and the stars. Fire and lightening and winds are there, and all that now is and all that is not.

There is no failure in the spiritual life, as long as we do not give up the fight.

The fulfillment of every desire only reveals its inadequacy.

The way upward from inertia to illumination passes through the sphere of action.

The breeze of God's grace is blowing continually. You have to set your sail to catch that breeze.

The mind is like a lake lashed into waves. Make the water of the lake clear and calm.
