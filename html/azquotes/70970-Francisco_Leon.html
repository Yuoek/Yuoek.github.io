They say reach for the stars and you will get to the moon. I say reach for yourself and you will get to the stars.

Embrace yourself, create a plan of action and of course, ponder the impact of your thoughts.

The greatest victory anyone can taste is the daily challenge to outrun those fears that you vanquished... days, weeks, months past.

Success will not be yours unless you measure your dream. And guess what? Once you have measured it, people will say it's too big for you. That's when you can say, 'That's not true, it's so small it even fits in my head'.

Do not think the world will become better in terms of our own inner change. We should rather think we'll achieve this goal only, and only if, we rescue the fact that the ultimate treasure lies at the end of our most positive creative deeds.

Will we ever understand the price of ambition, or ever apply our heart to life with submission?
