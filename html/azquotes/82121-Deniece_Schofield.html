Preventive Maintenance: Don't start today by doing yesterday's work.

Work at getting organized like a hobby. Set aside a certain amount of time each day (or whatever time your budget will allow). While it may indeed take a fair amount of time to establish order, once it is achieved, you will save more time than you have ever spent.

Even an organized person has days that aren't efficient and well-managed.

Being organized will give you more free time, contribute to a cheerful nature, and add to the peace and security of your home.

Decluttering tip: Ask yourself do I need this? How long has it been since I used this? Do I need so many?

Remember the law of the home: Junk expands to fill the space available, plus one room.
