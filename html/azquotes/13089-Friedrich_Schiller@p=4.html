Fate always wins, for our own heart within us Imperiously furthers its designs.

The universe is a thought of God.

Let no one despair, even though in the darkest night the last star of hope may disappear.

There is a nobility in the world of manners.

Freedom can occur only through education.

Art is the right hand of Nature.

Whatever lives, lives to die in sorrow. We engage our hearts, and grasp after the things of this world, only to undergo the pang of losing them.

You saw his weakness, and he will never forgive you.

Far must thy researches go
Wouldst thou learn the world to know;
Thou must tempt the dark abyss
Wouldst thou prove what Being is;
Naught but firmness gains the prize,
Naught but fullness makes us wise,
Buried deep truth e'er lies.

He who considers too much will perform little.

As freely as the firmament embraces the world, so mercy must encircle friend and foe. The sun poursforth impartially his beams through all the regions of infinity; heaven bestows the dew equally on every thirsty plant. Whatever is good and comes from on high is universal and without reserve: but in the heart's recesses darkness dwells.

An axe at home saves hiring a carpenter.

He who has done his best for his own time has lived for all times.

Illusion is brief, but repentance is long.

Man is an imitative creature.

Weep, for the light is dead.

Power is the most persuasive rhetoric.

Forgiveness is the divinest of victories.

Whatever is not forbidden is permitted.

What's old collapses, times change, And new life blossoms in the ruins.

Many a smiling face hides a mourning heart; but grief alone teaches us what we are.

O tender yearning, sweet hoping!
The golden time of first love!
The eye sees the open heaven,
The heart is intoxicated with bliss;
O that the beautiful time of young love
Could remain green forever.

Our safety is not in blindness, but in facing our dangers.

What is the short meaning of the long speech?
