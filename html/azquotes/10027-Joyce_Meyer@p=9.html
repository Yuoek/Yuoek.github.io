Joyce Meyer Quotes - Page 9

Prayer doesn't just change things - it changes us. If we are diligent in seeking God, slowly and surely we become better people.

I wonder how many times people give up just before a breakthrough - when they are on the very brink of success.

Jesus didn't die for us so we could pretend to be something we're not.

God has uniquely gifted you to help someone. Be available as God opens doors for you to do so.

Set your mind that no matter what is going on in your life, you will have a good attitude.

Treat everyone you meet as if they have infinite value because in God’s eyes they do

Passion is a decision about how we will approach life, before it becomes a feeling!

We must meditate on what God has done in our life instead of what we are still waiting on Him to do.

God forgives us and remembers our sin no more; therefore, stop remembering what God has forgotten.

Love is and has always been His idea. He came to love us and to teach us how to love. When we do this, life is exciting, beautiful and rewarding.

Nobody has a perfect life and it's entirely possible that if you want someone else's life they are busy wanting someone else's too–maybe even yours.

Some people think they have discernment when actually they are just suspicious. Suspicion comes out of the unrenewed mind; discernment comes out of the renewed spirit.

God works according to the law of gradual growth, so don't be discouraged if your progress seems slow.

When we face our fears, we can find our freedom.

If you want the great and mighty things God has for you, you must get to the root of anger and deal with it. Get rid of the masks and face the things that happened in your life that made you the way you are today. Admit that you can't change by yourself. Until the root is removed, it'll continue to produce one bad fruit after another.

The source of our stress isn't really difficulties, circumstances and situations-it's our attitude and approach toward them.

If we are hungry enough for God, we will find a way into His presence. We should be so hungry for the presence of God that we absolutely will not go out of our house or tackle any kind of project until we have spent some time with Him.

There’s nothing more dangerous to the kingdom of darkness than a believer with the Word of God in their mouth.

A #2 pencil and a dream can take you anywhere.

As believers, our joy and peace are not based in doing and achieving, but in believing. Joy and peace come as a result of building our relationship with the Lord.

If you want a testimony, you are going to have a test.

There is nothing as tragic as being alive and not enjoying life... Life should be celebrated!

God wants to use you to make other people happy! And the happier you make others, the happier you will be because you reap what you sow.

Satan frequently steals the will of God from us due to reasoning. The Lord may direct us to do a certain thing, but if it does not make sense - if it is not logical - we may be tempted to disregard it. What God leads a person to do does not always make logical sense to his mind. His spirit may affirm it and His mind reject it, especially if it would be out of the ordinary or unpleasant or if it would require personal sacrifice or discomfort.

Everything we run away from has power over us;everything we go through,we conquer.
