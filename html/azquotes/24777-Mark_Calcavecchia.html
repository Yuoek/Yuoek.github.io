Golf is just a game - and an idiotic game most of the time.

Hit it hard. It will land somewhere.

When I'm in a zone, I don't think about the shot or the wind or the distance or the gallery or anything; I just pull a club and swing.

Every day you gotta bring it and you gotta perform. If you have one big bad hole and just hit a couple of bad shots at the wrong time, pretty much wrecks the whole day and most cases your whole week. So that's the hard thing about golf.

If I had hit it like I wanted to I'd have holed it.
