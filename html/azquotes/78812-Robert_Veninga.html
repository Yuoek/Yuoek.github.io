Human pain does not let go of its grip at one point in time. Rather, it works its way out of our consciousness over time. There is a season of sadness. A season of anger. A season of tranquility. A season of hope.

When one has faith that the spring thaw will arrive, the winter winds seem to lose some of their punch.

A crisis event often explodes the illusions that anchor our lives.

Faith has a powerfull effect In helping people recover a sense of Balance, tranquililty and hope.
