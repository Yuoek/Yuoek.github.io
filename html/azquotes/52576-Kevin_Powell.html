Too many people are going for self and not trying to help their communities.

We live in a society where manhood is all about conquering and violence. And what we don't realize is that ultimately that kind of manhood ultimately kills you.

In an effort to reclaim our humanity, let us find new motivation for living by opening our minds to broader concepts of spirituality.

For our past can be a prison we are locked in permanently, or it can be the key to our freedom if we glean the lessons from it and deal with it directly.
