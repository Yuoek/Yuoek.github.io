I wanted to do an album that was very simple and very minimal in the context of instrumentation and stuff. I wanted to use as few means as possible to create a very powerful and interesting sound that would breathe.

I guess my life was in a pretty disorderly fashion, so I think the music came out that way a little bit.

It's not really a conscious decision to do a quiet album or a noisier album. It's just something that happens, I guess.
