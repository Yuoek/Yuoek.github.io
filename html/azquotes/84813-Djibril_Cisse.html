Italian football is great but for me, for the style of player I am, I found it too tactical, too slow.

My injury I told myself to deal with it, and bounce back stronger. I think I have.

As a player it is great to experience many other ways of playing, other tactics and other mentalities. I have played in several countries and because of that I know without any doubt that the English league is the one for me.

I love Liverpool. The people are wonderful and I feel very much at home there.
