Gautama Buddha Quotes - Page 2

What you have become is the result of what you have thought

If a string has one end, it has another.

If you find no one to support you on the spiritual path, walk alone. There is no companionship with the immature.

A person is ruined by taking the measure of other persons.

"All conditioned things are impermanent" - when one sees this with wisdom, one turns away from suffering.

Every experience, no matter how bad it seems, holds within a blessing of some kind. The goal is to find it.

If you sleep, Desire grows in you Like a vine in the forest. Like a monkey in the forest You jump from tree to tree, Never finding the fruit - From life to life, Never finding peace. If you are filled with desire Your sorrows swell Like the grass after the rain. But if you subdue desire Your sorrows shall fall from you Like drops of water from a lotus flower.

Nothing is forever except change.

When we free ourselves of desire, we will know serenity and freedom.

Those who are truly wise will remain unmoved by feelings of happiness and suffering, fame and disgrace, praise and blame, gain and loss.They will remain calm like the eye of a hurricane.

Be a lamp to yourself. Be your own confidence. Hold on to the truth within yourself as to the only truth.

If a man's mind becomes pure, his surroundings will also become pure.

When wishes are few, the heart is happy. When craving ends, there is peace.

Within this moment, the only moment that exists, the past, present and future are contained.

Crying with the wise is better than laughing with the fool.

Our life is a creation of our mind.

The mind that perceives the limitation is the limitation.

Forgive and be free. Forget that you have forgiven and be freer.

The greatest action is not conforming with the worlds ways.

Know well what holds you back, and what moves you forward

The secret of health for both mind and body is...live the present moment wisely and earnestly.

If you find someone with wisdom, good judgment, and good actions; make him a companion.

Conquer anger by love, evil by good; Conquer the miser with liberality, and the liar with truth.

Treat others with respect. How you treat others will be how they treat you.

Don't pray for lighter burdens, but for stronger backs.
