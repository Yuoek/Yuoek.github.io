I wasn’t a good student, and even now I never say that I am better than anybody, but I know I love Jiu Jitsu more than anybody. I love the energy and that it gets deeper the more you study.

I always try to attack. While I'm on the offensive, my opponent can think of nothing but defending.

When I step on the mat I know there is no other place I'd rather be.

Everyone is the same for the first two minutes, everyone has a chance to win, but after that you start to seperate physically and mentally.

The more you attack, the more your opponent will make mistakes.

Sometimes I just want to be with my family and watch movie and eat some popcorn. But when I step on the mat I know there is no other place I'd rather be.
