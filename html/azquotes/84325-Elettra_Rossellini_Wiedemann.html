Models are like athletes: You burn hard and fast.

I'm not an impulse shopper. I will wait three days after I see something. Am I still thinking about it? Then I'll get it.

If I'm going to be working out two hours a day, I may as well have a goal... and I'm pretty competitive by nature. A triathlon is a new fun thing.

I don't speak up if I'm working. As a model, no one pays me for my opinion. I want to wear whatever will make a great photo, whether I like it or not. But if the shoes are too small, I will complain!
