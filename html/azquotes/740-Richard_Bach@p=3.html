Richard Bach Quotes - Page 3

No matter how qualified or deserving we are, we will never reach a better life until we can imagine it for ourselves and allow ourselves to have it.

In order to win, you must expect to win.

Perspective - Use It or Lose It. If you turned to this page, you're forgetting that what is going on around you is not reality. Think about that.

We choose our next world through what we learn in this one. Learn nothing, and the next world is the same as this one, all the same limitations and lead weights to overcome.

If you love someone, set them free. If they come back they're yours; if they don't they never were.

You can achieve a goal; you never really achieve your purpose, because your purpose is ongoing. It never stops.

Every gift from a friend is a wish for your happiness.

Your friends will know you better in the first minute you meet than your acquaintances will know you in a thousand years.

You are led through your lifetime by the inner learning creature, the playful spiritual being that is your real self. Don't turn away from possible futures before you're certain you don't have anything to learn from them. You're always free to change your mind and choose a different future, or a different past.

Do you ever have the feeling you're a tourist on earth?

One must first learn to fall if one would fly.

Can miles truly separate you from friends... If you want to be with someone you love, aren't you already there?

Don't be dismayed by good-byes. A farewell is necessary before you can meet again. And meeting again, after moments or lifetimes, is certain for those who are friends.

Your conscience is the measure of the honesty of your selfishness. Listen to it carefully.

It's like, at the end, there's this surprise quiz: Am I proud of me? I gave my life to become the person I am right now. Was it worth what I paid?

Life's battles don't always go to the strongest or fastest; sooner or later those who win are those who think they can.

Listen to what you know instead of what you fear.

Learning is finding out what you already know. Doing is demonstrating that you know it. Teaching is reminding others that they know just as well as you. You are all learners, doers, teachers.

You teach best what you most need to learn.

Our soulmate is the one who makes life come to life.

Hate is love without enough data.

Don't believe what your eyes are telling you. All they show is limitation. Look with your understanding, find out what you already know, and you'll see the way to fly.

Just be who you are, calm and clear and bright. Automatically, as we shine who we are, asking ourselves every minute is this what I really want to do, doing it only when we answer yes, automatically that turns away those who have nothing to learn from who we are and attracts those who do, and from whom we have to learn, as well.

To fly as fast as thought, to anywhere that is, you must begin by knowing that you have already arrived.

Thought plus belief equals manifestation.
