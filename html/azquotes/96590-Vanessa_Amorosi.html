I guess my mantra is you have to work really hard if you want something. You have to be committed to hard work and persistence even when it gets tough.

Touring and performing is the best part of my job. I love it. I guess I like pop and rock. Really getting into a powerful stage performane, running around like crazy on the stage.

I started working in music very young, I was raised by a family of musicians and performers, so I guess it was more second nature than making an actual decision.

Writing 'This Is Who I Am...' I wanted to write a song about where I'm at, that I have accepted where I have come to and I wanted my fans to be able to connect to it too. I want them to stand proud!
