No one could tell me where my soul might be;
I searched for God but he eluded me;
I sought my brother out and found all three.

We are humans who see clearly the barbarity of all ages except our own.

The great unity which true science seeks is found only by beginning with our knowledge of God, and coming down from Him along the stream of causation to every fact and event that affects us.

A strange lot this, to be dropped down in a world of barbarians - men who see clearly enough the barbarity of all ages except their own.
