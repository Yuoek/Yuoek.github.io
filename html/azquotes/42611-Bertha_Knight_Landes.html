In politics it commonly takes a superior woman to overcome the handicap of traditional prejudice.

Since politics — until recently — has been a man’s world, men, as a whole are responsible for its corruption.

Let us, while never forgetting our womanhood, drop all emphasis on sex, and put it on being public servants.

Women now wield considerable power along political lines and I believe each succeeding year for some time to come will find them wielding that power more effectively.
