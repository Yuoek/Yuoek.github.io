We never really did anything just for fun.

Lyrically, I feel like there are things I always go back to, having to do with relationships.

The hardest part was knowing that I would have to talk about the album when I released it. I think the hardest part was waiting to see how long it would take for me to be able to get through the questions people would ask me.

Every relationship has its drama and baggage.

I wanted to protect the songs. I wanted to make sure I could write freely and not be self-conscious about it.
