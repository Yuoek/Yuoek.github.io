Those you cannot teach to fly, teach to fall faster.

Madness is not a consequence of uncertainty but of certainty.

What was a lie in the father becomes a conviction in the son.

Become who you are!

Every man has his price. This is not true. But for every man there exists a bait which he cannot resist swallowing.

O my brothers, am I then cruel? But I say: that which is falling should also be pushed!

Whatever harm the evil may do, the harm done by the good is the most harmful harm.

People live for the morrow, because the day-after-to-morrow is doubtful.

Remorse.-- Never yield to remorse, but at once tell yourself: remorse would simply mean adding to the first act of stupidity a second.

Truths are illlusions which we have forgotten are illusions.

The people we keep standing in the anteroom of our favor either start fermenting or turn sour.

This is what is hardest: to close the open hand because one loves.

The love of power is the demon of mankind.

One must repay good and ill; but why just to the person who did us good or ill?

The secret of reaping the greatest fruitfulness and the greatest enjoyment from life is to live dangerously.

Happiness is a fata morgana. the only way to not end up unhappy is to not long for happiness.

There is a rollicking kindness that looks like malice.

Some die too young, some die too old; the precept sounds strange, but die at the right age.

In solitude the lonely man is eaten up by himself, among crowds by the many.

All idealism is falsehood in the face of necessity.

He who is punished is never he who performed the deed. He is always the scapegoat.

By losing your goal, You have lost your way.

To the mediocre, mediocrity is a form of happiness.

Shared joys make a friend, not shared sufferings.

A thinker sees his own actions as experiments and questions--as attempts to find out something. Success and failure are for him answers above all.
