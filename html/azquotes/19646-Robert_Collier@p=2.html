Robert Collier Quotes - Page 2

Your belief that you can do the thing gives your thought forces their power.

The mere fact that you have obstacles to overcome is in your favor.

If you see yourself as prosperous, you will be. If you see yourself as continually hard up, that is exactly what you will be.

All of us have bad luck and good luck. The man who persists through the bad luck - who keeps right on going - is the man who is there when the good luck comes - and is ready to receive it.

If you procrastinate when faced with a big difficult problem... break the problem into parts, and handle one part at a time.

Begin to free yourself at once by doing all that is possible with the means you have, and as you proceed in this spirit the way will open for you to do more.

Very few persons, comparatively, know how to Desire with sufficient intensity. They do not know what it is to feel and manifest that intense, eager, longing, craving, insistent, demanding, ravenous Desire which is akin to the persistent, insistent, ardent, overwhelming desire of the drowning man for a breath of air; of the shipwrecked or desert-lost man for a drink of water; of the famished man for bread and meat.

In every adversity there lies the seed of an equivalent advantage. In every defeat is a lesson showing you how to win the victory next time.

Any thought that is passed on to the subconscious often enough and convincingly enough is finally accepted.

Make every thought, every fact, that comes into your mind pay you a profit. Make it work and produce for you. Think of things not as they are but as they might be. Don't merely dream - - but create!

All power is from within and therefore under our control

But fear - panic - knows no reason. It brings into being overnight the things that it fears. It is the greatest torment of humanity. Fear is, in short, the devil. It causes most of the sin, disaster, disease and misery of the world. It is the ravening lion roaming the earth seeking whom it may...devour. The only refuge is in the knowledge that it has no power other than the power you give it.

You can have what you want- if you know how to form the mold for it in your own thoughts. There is no dream that may not come true, if you but learn to use the Creative Force working through you. The methods that work for one will work for all. The key to power lies in using what you have...freely, fully and thus opening wide your channels for more creative force to flow through you.

The first essentials, of course, is to know what you want.

The power for happiness, for good, for everything we need of life is within each one of us. The power is there - unlimited power.

People blame their environment, their education, their opportunities, their luck, for their condition. They are wrong. There is one person to blame — and only one — THEMSELVES.

The great thing is the start - to see an opportunity for service, and to start doing it, even though in the beginning you serve but a single customer - and him for nothing.

Knowing that you have working with you a force, which never yet has failed in anything it has undertaken, you can go ahead in the confident knowledge that it will not fail in your case, either.

It is your work to clear away the mass of encumbering material of thoughts, so that you may bring into plain view the precious thing at the center of the mass.

Always enter the conversation already taking place in the customer's mind.

Supply always comes on the heels of demand.

As fast as each opportunity presents itself, use it! No matter how tiny an opportunity it may be, use it!

Plant the seed of desire in your mind and it forms a nucleus with power to attract to itself everything needed for its fulfillment.

A single idea - the sudden flash of a thought - may be worth a million dollars.

Constant repetition carries conviction.
