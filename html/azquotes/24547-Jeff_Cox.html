A garden is a love song, a duet between a human being and Mother Nature.

Confronted with the vision of a beautiful garden, we see something beautiful about ourselves.

A person who loves his or her work Is like a plant in the right spot: There growth is maximized And the yield is greatest.

Our notion of what makes a paradise always returns to the image of a beautiful and fruitful garden.

Fresh grapes and wine are perhaps the most luscious foods we mortals encounter during our sojourn here.
