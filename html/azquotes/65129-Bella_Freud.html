Ever since I was little I really loved boyish clothes - I had a real obsession with strict clothes, like uniforms. They really got me going.

My earliest influences were Coco Chanel and Yves Saint Laurent.

I love a star print. I always get a lurch if I see a nice one.

I grew up in the countryside, and I was obsessed with horses and wildlife.

Somehow, stars have a lot of class if they are done right; they never have a shelf life.
