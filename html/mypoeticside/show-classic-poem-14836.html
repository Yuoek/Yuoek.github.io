Lol of the Wild Yellow Rose

Habba Khatoon

Wild, the vagrant yellow rose
Again has bloomed,
Beauty has in all that grows
Rare forms assumed!
Where, O love, your hiding place ?
I wander far,
Seeking you among the streams
The dew-drops pour.
Jasmine in the forest gleams,
But where your face ?
Violets bloom for me to trace
To where you are.
