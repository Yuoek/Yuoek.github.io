The Stationmasters Garden

Reginald Arkell

Our Stationmaster's garden is particularly fine,
It's rather like those landscapes that they hang upon the line;
And all the railway passengers put out their heads to say:
"The stationmaster s's garden's looking very bright today".

Our stationmaster's garden is the favourite on the rails,
And all the railway passengers to Paddington or Wales,
Smile across at one another, in their carriages and say:
"The stationmaster's garden's looking very bright today".
