Zema Arayam

Saint Yared

Hale luya le-Ab
Hale luya le-wold
Hale luya le- Menfes Kidus
kedamiha le-Tsion
Semaye sarere webedagim arayo le-tsion le-Muse
Ze-Keme yigebir gebra le-debtera



English translation:


Praise be to the Father
Praise be to the Son
Praise be to the Holy Ghost
Prior to Zion God created the Heavens
God showed Moses the tent
